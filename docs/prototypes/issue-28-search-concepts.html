Issue #28 — One location-aware search field

Interactive design prototype (mobile target, 380 px). Tap the GPS ⌖ buttons, the location ✕, and the chips — they react. This is a visual concept, not the live app. Strings are the real DE i18n strings. Recommended options are tagged.

Per your direction: the bottom footer tabs (Ergebnisse / Karte / Filter) stay. The two big top tabs (“Überall / In der Nähe”) are what we remove — see the Before below, then the concepts.

Before — today's two-tab layout

The mode tabs sit above the field; the chip strip mixes amenities, “Alle” and categories in one undifferentiated row.
